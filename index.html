Web Design

Experienced in the field of Web Design for 5+ years in such areas as ecommerce, fintech, medicine, religion, education, infographics and much more. I’m excited by the large projects from the ground up, constructing the wireframes, testing the prototypes. I give bring new life to the hopelessly outdated interfaces, deeply immersing myself into the history and philosophy of the project and also give my clients my support in the future. Animations

Today, modern technologies allow us to animate static web pages and applications using program code, so we can get rid of heavy video and GIF formats. Now our animations look more beautiful and uncompressed at any scale. For myself, I choose the great library - Lottie. My whole process of creating animation is divided into the following steps: I make vector illustration, import it into Adobe After Effects and work on my animation here. In the end I get a code of the animation, which can easily be implemented into the website or mobile application.
